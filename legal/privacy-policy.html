Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Simple Visual Noise ("we", "us", "the app") is developed by William Jiamin. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Simple Visual Noise operates entirely on your device. We do not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your preferences locally on your device using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults). This includes:

  • Selected theme/skin preference
  • Visualizer settings (waveform style, colors, animation speed)
  • Background video assignments per mode
  • Video opacity preference
  • Background mode preference

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. In-App Purchases

Premium features are available as a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not process any payment information directly. Apple's privacy policy governs how payment data is handled: apple.com/legal/privacy

4. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or device identifiers. There are no cookies, pixels, or third-party trackers.

5. Audio Processing

All audio is generated procedurally on your device or played from bundled public domain recordings. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored beyond real-time playback. The app does not access your microphone.

6. Third-Party Services

Simple Visual Noise does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or servers. The app works fully offline.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
